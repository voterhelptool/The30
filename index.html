UPHOLD

The rights of the people vs. the machinery of indifference

You hold the 30 universal human rights in your hand. The Bureaucrat is playing violations.
Counter each one. Defend what belongs to everyone.
Empty your hand first. Uphold the 30.

HOW TO PLAY

UPHOLD THE 30

🎯 YOUR GOAL
Counter violations by playing the matching rights card. When all violations are played, if you have more correct answers than wrong ones — you win. Every right answer is one right upheld.
🃏 ON YOUR TURN
The Bureaucrat plays a Violation — a situation where a human right is being threatened. Play the matching Rights card from your hand to counter it.
⚠️ WRONG CARD = PENALTY
Play the wrong rights card and you draw a penalty card — your hand gets bigger and harder to empty. Think before you play.
📥 DON'T HAVE THE CARD?
Hit DRAW & SKIP to pull a new card. Each draw costs you one point — it counts as a wrong. Keep drawing until you find the right card. You'll always get there eventually.
✨ SPECIAL CARDS
🛡 SHIELD — Block any violation, no match needed.
⚡ ESCALATE — Redirect the violation, remove 2 from the Bureaucrat's hand.
✌️ SOLIDARITY — Play 2 rights at once, clear 2 cards.
🚫 NOPE — Cancel the Bureaucrat's next special move.
